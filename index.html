Why Therapy?

Therapy is a powerful invitation into self-exploration, healing, and personal growth. It’s a place where you can slow down, be heard, and rewire the way you meet your world.

  • Understand and transform the challenges that hold you back.
  • Break free from negative patterns and cultivate healthier habits.
  • Gain clarity, confidence, and resilience for life’s evolutions.

Using modalities such as NLP, Reverse Therapy, Solution-Oriented Therapy, and Positive Psychology, every session is crafted to meet you exactly where you are — and to open the possibility of where you want to go. Together, we’ll explore what’s keeping you stuck and unlock the possibilities for a brighter, more fulfilling future.

A unique approach to therapy

My work is deeply inspired by Milton Erickson, the pioneer of medical hypnosis and family therapy whose methods continue to shape modern hypnotherapy.

Training with Stephen Gilligan, Bill O’Hanlon, Dr. Richard Bandler, and Paul McKenna allows me to integrate Ericksonian techniques, NLP, and Positive Psychology into every session.

Whatever you are navigating — chronic fatigue, addiction, relationship difficulties, or emotional blocks — I’ll stand by you with guidance and tools to reclaim your life.
